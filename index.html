Kidney Stone Detection System

Early identification based on symptoms and risk indicators

Problem Statement

Kidney stones are solid mineral and salt deposits that form in the kidneys and can cause severe pain, urinary issues, and long-term complications if not detected early. Many individuals ignore early symptoms, leading to delayed diagnosis and treatment. This system helps users assess the likelihood of kidney stones using common clinical indicators.

Prevention & Awareness

✔ Drink sufficient water daily

✔ Reduce excess salt intake

✔ Avoid high oxalate foods if prone

✔ Maintain healthy body weight

✔ Seek medical advice for persistent pain
